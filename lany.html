About LANY

LANY (an acronym for "Los Angeles New York") is an American Pop Band. Known for their dreamy synths and emotional lyrics, they’ve gained a global following with their unique sound. They had a recent tour in the Philippines, with one night at the Philippine Arena on October 12, 2024, and three nights in Cebu from October 13-15. As a fan, I attended their show in Cebu on Day 1.
